Is art illustration of beauty and harmony, revelation of divine will, formal expression of artists sensual experiences or aesthetic experimental research? As in case of many other fields, art redefines its role in culture. In last century art aggressively contested a faith as primary spiritual experience, and coupled with philosophy in a search of deep intellectual meaning. Entering public domain art faced competition from entertainment as the guiding skipper of ideal public model. Art objects supposed to radiate meaning and establish aesthetic experience between and object/event and a spectator/participant. Whereas authors might have the intent and a narrative they can't cover every possible meaning that the object/event create. That freedom to interpret the meaning of art by a spectator/participant creates freedom of finding the meaning to everyone. To facilitate such freedom of reading an art objects tend to become more ambiguous, subtly whispering many stories.
