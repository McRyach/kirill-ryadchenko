DISCIPLINARY ENTROPY

A discipline evloves and changes its course. In a historic perspective it may have week connections with its original role, but it compensate through finding new ones. This trajectory deviates with the social image of the discipline due to its necessary autonomy. When discipline is allowed to enclose itself and receive a budget of credibility from a public. It allows discipline to focus on it's internal problems, increasing organizational complexity, and forming a conceptual theory. Such enclosure allows greater organizational complxity and richness, but requires to proof its credibility before public.
